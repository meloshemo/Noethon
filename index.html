How It Works

Connect the data you already have, then turn it into one decision layer.

Modern B2B utility software should say exactly what it does: read signals, score risk and route action.

01

Ingest utility signals

Read telemetry, alarms, meter events, asset context and work queues from systems utilities already operate.

02

Score risk and predict events

Use rules and simulation models to rank leak risk, pressure drift, load spikes and response priorities.

03

Route the right action

Push one prioritized queue to operators and field teams so decisions happen faster and with less manual coordination.
